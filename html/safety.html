Our Pledge.

Evo's top priority is the safety and well-being of out guests, our team members, and each and every individual of our community. To keep us all safe, we have implemented health and safety protocols that go above and beyond our already strict and regulated standards.

Required safety equipments.

We require our staff to wear face masks. Guests are also required to wear face masks when you arrive or get up from a table.

Frequent hand washing.

We follow strict and thorough handwashing guidelines issued by the FDA and CDC.

Social distancing measures.

Tables are spaced following all social distancing guidelines..
